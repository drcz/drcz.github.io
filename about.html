My name is Michał Jędrzej Stańczyk but family, friends and allies call me dercz. I often go by the name Ścisław Dercz and sporadically some other ones. I was born in Gdańsk, Poland on 1984, and since 2015 I live in Alicante, Spain. Since 2013 I'm a b.sc. in applied mathematics (Technical University of Gdansk). For roughly a decade I worked as an NLP researcher and a programmer which my cv confirms, so that must be true. Since June 2024 I'm an aspiring backend engineer in a lovely Spanish startup belender.
